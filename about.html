About St. Xaviers High School

A Legacy of Excellence in Tirtol since 2000

Our Heritage

Founded with a vision to provide world-class education in the heart of Koraput, St. Xaviers High School Tirtol has evolved into a premier co-educational institution. We are committed to nurturing global citizens who are rooted in traditional Indian values.

Our campus provides a perfect blend of modern infrastructure and a serene environment conducive to learning and personal growth.

👁️

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence that empowers students with 21st-century skills, fostering innovation, empathy, and integrity.

🚀

Our Mission

To provide a holistic learning environment that encourages academic rigor, artistic exploration, and physical well-being through personalized mentorship.

The Xavierian Pillars

Academic Rigor

Following the CBSE curriculum with an emphasis on conceptual clarity and critical thinking.

Technology Integration

Smart labs and AI-assisted learning modules to prepare students for a digital future.

Character Building

Focusing on "Value Education" to instill honesty, respect, and social responsibility.

Global Outlook

Encouraging students to participate in international Olympiads and cultural exchange programs.

Our Teaching Methodology

At St. Xaviers, we follow the 3-E Approach: Explore, Experience, and Excel. We move beyond rote memorization to activity-based learning, ensuring every student understands the 'Why' and 'How' behind every concept.
