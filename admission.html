Admission 2026-27

Admission Procedure

Registration for the new academic session starts in January every year. Parents can collect the prospectus and registration form from the school office between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

  • Step 1: Submission of Registration Form.
  • Step 2: Interactive session/Aptitude Test.
  • Step 3: Document Verification.
  • Step 4: Fee Deposition.

Documents Required

  • Original Birth Certificate.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school.
  • Passport size photographs (4 Nos).
  • Aadhar Card copy of Student and Parents.
  • Previous Year's Report Card.
